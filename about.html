About Us




Established in 2008, as Go Jo Recycled Clothing, our mission from day one was to reinvent how you shop for second hand clothing.

We stock an eclectic emporium of clothing, shoes, and accessories - from retro through to designer. We pride ourselves on ensuring you find the pieces you need to complete the outfit you are looking for, whether it's for every day use, or that special occassion.

Extra care is taken to ensure our clothing and accessories are in excellent condition when they hit the shop floor, and we will be more than happy to show you through our range. We are here to help you find exactly what you are looking for.

All of our items are carefully selected, and a lot of our clothing comes from personal collections. We relish the chance to bring these items along their journey to their new owner, and if you have a collection you think we may be interested in we would love to hear from you.

We look forward to seeing you in store! - Jo
